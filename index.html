About Us

Your Admissions Counselor:
Parul Gaur

I have always loved helping people, especially in one-on-one settings. For years I worked as a nutrition therapist, a credentialed teacher in California, and helped many students as a tutor and by giving them college advice.

However, when I went through the college admissions process with my son, I was shocked by the high fees, cookie-cutter methodology, and focus on counseling hours instead of quality applications in the college counseling industry.

I decided to change this with USA Admit. After getting my own son into Stanford University, I started helping other students and found that, using my talents, I could provide a better alternative to mainstream counseling services.

For you, I will untangle this complex admissions process into an exciting, stress-free journey that will lead you to a higher-education experience that is right for you.

Services

USA Admit offers specialized counseling to students looking to apply to some of the United States' top Universities. Our private counseling sessions and essay revisions will give you help you best present the work you have done so far to maximize your chance of admission to the institutions of your dreams in programs that best suit your interests and future plans.
