About Us

Your Admissions Counselor:
Parul Gaur

USA Admit was born four years ago when I was looking for a college admissions counselor for my son. I found that they were too cookie-cutterish. I felt that it was difficult for students to stay creative when paying hourly for essay help meant that they were always racing against time.

Right away, I knew that I could use my years' of experience with high schoolers as a credentialed teacher in California to better help them in admissions.

I am knowledgable about the potential pitfalls in college essay writing and have a strong ability to keep them motivated throughout the rigorous application period.

From the start, my coaching helped my students gain admission to prestigious universities. I am very passionate about my work: nothing feels better than knowing I was able to help make a student's future a little brighter.

College Applications Counseling


It all starts with finding the perfect match.

We will help you navigate the enormous number of potential universities, programs, and applications to find the right set of colleges for you.

Then, with our specialized counseling and essay revisions, we will help you best present the work you have done so far. You can be sure that every detail has been carefully considered and we will be there for you any time of day, seven days a week.




Test Prep


We will help you navigate the stress of testing and get the top scores you need to be considered for the most selective universities in America. We have specific expertise in countless AP exams and our tutors have had stellar success in the US testing gauntlet.

At every step of the way, we will be there to work through the problems with you.




High School Planning


We will help you pick the best courses and extracurricular activities to create a strong foundation for your college application.

This will allow you to form a coherent picture of your future while enjoying and making the most out of high school.
